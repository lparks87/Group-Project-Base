The API I have created will have a home page that will allow the stakeholders to be able to apply the necessary filters needed. I think this system will not only help the stakeholders make a profit but maybe it will help them produce higher quality movies. They will be able to determine what duration, viewer rating, and genre result in the greatest number of invoices or the highest total.

The main stakeholders would include the corresponding companies that feature the queries movie or tv-show. For instance, if a company is looking to make more of a profit, they can query to determine what movies have the greatest number of invoices or highest total of invoices. Content platforms may want to use this platform to determine if they want a contract with a specific studio based on the profit, they will bring in. Production companies may want to use this platform to determine what viewer rating, genre, or studio performs the best, so they can produce more content in the same realm.
